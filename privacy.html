Privacy Policy

Last Updated — March 2026

1. Introduction

Avocabo ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage data such as words viewed, categories selected, streak counts, and feature engagement. This helps us improve the App experience.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility purposes.

Subscription Data: Your subscription status is managed through RevenueCat and Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Process subscriptions through Apple's App Store
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Send local notifications for streak reminders and new words
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve content and features

4. Data Storage & Security

Your vocabulary preferences, favorites, streaks, and progress are stored locally on your device. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit — for subscription management
  • RevenueCat — for subscription analytics and management

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

6. Subscription Data

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription status is verified through Apple's StoreKit framework and RevenueCat.

7. Children's Privacy

Avocabo is an educational app suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of anonymized analytics

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at avocabolab@gmail.com

9. Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data. Anonymized usage data may be retained for analytical purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
